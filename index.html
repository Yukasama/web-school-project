Product Line

Oblivion was designed to observe every strength of many powerful cryptocurrencies all over the world and combine them into one single cryptocurrency: Oblivion.
A very known strength of, for example, Solana, is it's incredible scalability and speed in transaction handling. While it can process up to 65.000 thousand transactions per second, the fees to make a transaction are substantially lower than e.g. in Ethereum. If that wouldn't be enough, the typical transaction duration is about 2 seconds, which is incredibly fast aswell. There's one problem though, due to Solanas speed and scalability it can be harmed easier by hackers, because it's not very dezentralized.
This is called the Blockchain Trilemma.
